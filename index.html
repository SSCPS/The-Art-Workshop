Welcome to the Art Workshop

The Art Workshop is a program in South Shore Charter Public School that allows its students to expand on their abilities and test their skills as artists. Here at the Art Workshop we make works of art and showcase them in contests to compete nationwide, including the Boston Globe All Scholastic Art Awards!
