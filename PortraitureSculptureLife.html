Portraiture, Sculpture, & Life Art

Here at the Portraiture, Sculpture, & Life students create art by either creating portraiture, sculptures, or drawings through still life. These projects are especially challenging because of the challenge of drawing the intricacies of the human face, turning ideas into 3D sculptures, and creating art with different mediums for still life.
